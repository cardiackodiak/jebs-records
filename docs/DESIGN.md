# Jeb's Records

## Purpose

Jeb's Records is a digital companion to a living room listening space.

It should make choosing and enjoying vinyl feel effortless.

---

## Principles

- The album cover is the hero.
- The interface should disappear when not needed.
- Motion should be calm and subtle.
- Every element earns its place.
- Design for the couch, not the desk.
- Modern, understated, timeless.

---

## Ambient Mode

When idle, the site should become part of the room.

The music remains.
The interface fades away.
